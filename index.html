CDTFA-240-A

Spirits received from your own bottling or packaging department. Must agree with TTB federal forms. (Regulation 2504)

CDTFA-241-A

Spirits purchased or received from other CA licensed manufacturers, importers, or wholesalers. Include returns from CA wholesalers. (Regulation 2505)

9-digit number, no dashes
CDTFA-242-A

Spirits imported into California from out-of-state or Foreign Trade Zone. Report at time of importation, not withdrawal from bond. (Regulation 2506)

J=Truck, R=Rail, S=Ship
CDTFA-243-B

Exempt sales / deliveries to other CA licensed manufacturers, importers, or wholesalers. Do NOT include sales to CA retailers — those are taxable. Also include own-branch transfers and returns to vendors. (Regulation 2507)

9-digit, numbers only
CDTFA-244-B

Spirits exported from California, sold to common carriers in interstate service, or sold to Armed Forces instrumentalities. Exports must be actually exported within 90 days of sale.

EDS Transaction Type Reference

Use these codes when building your CSV/XLS import or mapping columns

PURCHASE — Spirits purchased from a licensed distributor
DISTILLED — Spirits distilled on your premises this month
RECTIFIED — Spirits blended or rectified on premises
BOTTLED — Bulk spirits transferred into bottles
SALE_DIST — Sale to a licensed Florida distributor
SALE_CONS — Gift shop or tasting room sale to consumer
TRANSFER — Transfer from bulk to gift shop inventory
EXPORT — Exported outside of Florida
TASTING — Samples given at tasting room / events
LOSS — Breakage, evaporation ("angel's share"), or waste

All bulk spirit quantities are in proof gallons. Formula: Gallons × Proof ÷ 100 = Proof Gallons. Distillery inventory at 100 proof baseline.

Ending balance from last month
Formula: Begin + Received + Distilled − To Bottling − To Case Goods − Loss = Ending

Bottling activity converts proof gallons → wine gallons for tax purposes. Wine Gal = Proof Gal ÷ Proof. These ABV tiers determine the Florida excise tax rate per §565.12 F.S.

Taxable Categories (Wine Gallons) — §565.12 F.S.

Rate: $2.25/gal (ch. 564 F.S.)
Rate: $6.50/gal (§565.12(1) F.S.)
Rate: $9.53/gal (§565.12(2) F.S.)

Case goods are bottled spirits tracked in gallons (wine gallons). A craft distillery may sell directly to consumers per §565.03(2) up to 75,000 gallons/year from its gift shop/tasting room.
